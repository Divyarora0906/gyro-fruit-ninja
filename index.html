FAQ

Questions Answered

Does it work on iPhone? ▾
Yes — but iOS requires Safari and you may need to grant motion sensor permission when prompted. Chrome on Android works out of the box.
Do I need to be on the same WiFi? ▾
No! Your phone and computer connect via our signaling server over the internet. Different networks work fine.
Is it multiplayer? ▾
Currently one phone controls one blade in the arena. Multiplayer mode with multiple phones is on the roadmap.
Is it free? ▾
Completely free. No sign-up, no subscription, no ads. Just open and play.
What browser should I use? ▾
Chrome or Edge on desktop for the best experience. Firefox works too. For the phone controller, Safari on iOS or Chrome on Android.
