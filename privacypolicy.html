Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

BLADENINJA does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. Your device sensor data never leaves your device beyond what is needed to control the game in real time.

This policy explains what data is accessed during gameplay, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Device Sensor Data

BLADENINJA uses your phone's built-in gyroscope and motion sensors solely to translate physical movement into in-game blade controls. Specifically:

  • Gyroscope orientation data (tilt, rotation) is read in real time.
  • Accelerometer data may be used to detect motion speed and direction.
  • This data is transmitted over a temporary, session-only WebSocket connection to the game host.
  • Sensor data is never stored, logged to a database, or sent to any third-party server.
  • All data is discarded the moment your game session ends.

Room & Session Data

When you create or join a game room, a temporary Room ID is generated to link your phone to the game arena. This ID:

  • Exists only for the duration of your session.
  • Is not linked to your identity, account, or device fingerprint.
  • Is automatically destroyed when the session ends or the browser tab is closed.

Cookies & Tracking

BLADENINJA does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Meta Pixel). No data is collected about how you navigate or interact with this site beyond what is needed to run the game.

Third-Party Services

The game loads the following external libraries to function:

  • Phaser.js (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — game engine
  • QRCode.js (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — QR code generation
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — typography

These services may log your IP address as part of their standard CDN operations. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Children's Privacy

BLADENINJA is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults. If you believe any data has been collected in error, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you are located in the EU (GDPR) or India (DPDP Act) and have concerns, you are welcome to reach out and we will respond promptly.
